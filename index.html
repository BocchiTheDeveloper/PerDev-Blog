Reflection

During this semester, I have learned a lot of things. These activities have taught me about what I am and how I can improve myself and become a better person. I have learned that my ideal self is to be someone who knows how to socialize with other people while at the same time staying at my comfort zone, and someone who is smart enough to make better decisions in life. But I learned that my actual self is someone who is mostly quiet, and does not socialize with people most often. I'm not good with decisions and I make a lot of mistakes in my life. August 8, 2024

This was when we were tasked to create an 8-segment-wheel that describes ourselves physically, emotionally, interactively, sensually, intellectually, nutritionally, spiritually, and environmentally. The wheel also has 3 layers. The outermost layer is for what your friends think of you, the second inner layer is for what your parents or close relatives think of you, and the innermost layer is for what you think of yourself. During this activity, I have learned that a lot of people see you differently than what you think you are, and only you can understand who you truly are.

August 14, 2024

What are the Ingredients for Success?

In order to achieve your dreams and to have a successful life, you must have the following ingredients:
1. Hard work, because nothing in life will ever be easy. There will be many challenges ahead which is why you must do your best and work hard to overcome them.
2. Optimism, because although the problems you face will be tough, you can't always have a sad face while trying to overcome them. Overcome your problems with a smile to reassure yourself and others around you that everything will be okay in the end.
3. Learning for mistakes. How else would you pickimprove if you don't learn from mistakes? in life, mistakes are very common. But what you should do is learn from them instead of letting them get to you.
4. Never giving up. Loss is normal, but what we should do about them is to keep getting back up. There's no limit to how much you can try again, that's why if you keep losing, try and try again until you reach your dreams.
5. Become a better person. Make good decisions, learn to love yourself and others, and most importantly, trust in God for He has plans for you, plans that are more than you can imagine. Trust in his plans, ask for his guidance and continue living your life and become a better person.
